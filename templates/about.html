Origin

How it all began.

Communication is fundamentally human. Yet for 70+ million deaf and hard-of-hearing people worldwide, language barriers persist in the digital age.

HandSignify started as a challenge: What if AI could understand sign language? What if technology could be an inclusive force for positive change?

Today, we're answering that challenge with code, creativity, and unwavering commitment to accessibility.
