About HandSignify

Empowering the deaf and hard-of-hearing community through advanced AI-driven translation technologies.

Our Mission

At HandSignify, we believe that communication should be a bridge, not a barrier. Our mission is to leverage the power of Computer Vision and Artificial Intelligence to create seamless, real-time translation between Sign Language and spoken or written languages.

We are dedicated to building tools that foster inclusivity, accessibility, and understanding across diverse communities worldwide.

Core Values

  • Accessibility: Technology for everyone.
  • Innovation: Pushing the boundaries of AI.
  • Impact: Creating real-world change.
  • Simplicity: Elegant and intuitive design.
