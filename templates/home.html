Flow

From camera frame to meaningful output.

Flask · Socket.IO · MediaPipe · Random Forest — wired for clarity and speed.

1

Capture

Frames stream from the browser into the backend MJPEG endpoint.

2

Interpret

MediaPipe extracts 21-point hand landmarks fed into a trained Random Forest model.

3

Deliver

Predictions stream back via WebSocket and update the UI as text, signs, or speech.
