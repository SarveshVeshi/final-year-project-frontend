FAQ

Do I need to give camera permissions?
Yes, for Sign ‚Üí Text and Sign ‚Üí Voice, your browser will ask for camera permission on first use. This is processed locally and never stored on our servers. You have full control over permissions.
Is my data private?
Yes, all data is processed in real-time on your device. We do not store or transmit your camera footage or audio outside your browser unless you explicitly share it. Your privacy is our priority.
What languages are supported?
Currently, we support American Sign Language (ASL) and Indian Sign Language (ISL) for primary conversions. Voice recognition supports multiple spoken languages depending on your browser locale.
Does it work on mobile devices?
Yes! All converters work on mobile devices. However, for best results with camera features, use a device with a good camera and stable internet connection.
How accurate is the recognition?
Our AI model achieves ~90%+ accuracy for clear sign recognition. Accuracy improves with good lighting, clear hand visibility, and steady hand movements. The system learns from usage patterns.
Found a bug? How do I report it?
Found an issue? Please reach out to our team using the contact information below. We appreciate your feedback and will address bugs as soon as possible!
